3D printing

3D printing description

3D printing is an advanced manufacturing technology that uses computer-generated three-dimensional models to directly produce solid objects by layering materials. Compared with traditional manufacturing methods, 3D printing has many unique advantages. First of all, it can produce items with complex shapes, precisely replicating both internal structures and exterior designs.
